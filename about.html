About Me

I am an ecologist broadly interested in disturbance ecology and land management, specifically interested in fire ecology and restoring fire to landscapes. I have a B.S. in Biology from William and Mary and an M.S. in Sustainable Forest Management from Oregon State University.

I am currently working as a fuels monitoring module leader for The Ember Alliance, based in Greenville, SC. Outside of work, I play ultimate frisbee and enjoy making art and baking.
